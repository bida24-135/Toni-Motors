2025 McLaren P1

The McLaren P1 is a hybrid hypercar that combines a 3.8L twin-turbocharged V8 engine with an electric motor, delivering a combined output of 903 horsepower. The car features a lightweight carbon fiber monocoque chassis and active aerodynamics, including an adjustable rear wing that generates up to 1,323 pounds of downforce.
